Faculty Upgradation Program

Welcome to our Faculty Upgradation Program. This program is designed to enhance the skills and knowledge of our faculty members, ensuring that they stay up-to-date with the latest developments in their fields.

Program Objectives:

  • Stay current with industry trends and research.
  • Enhance teaching and mentoring abilities.
  • Promote research and publication among faculty.

Program Benefits:

  • Access to cutting-edge resources and training materials.
  • Mentorship from experienced faculty and experts.
  • Opportunities for research grants and collaboration.
